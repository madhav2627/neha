Survey Introduction:

We invite you to participate in this survey aimed at understanding the experiences, challenges, and contributions of working women in today's society. As more women join the workforce across various sectors, it is important to explore how their roles are evolving, what obstacles they face, and how society perceives and supports their participation in professional life. Your responses will help us gain valuable insights into these issues and contribute to informed discussions on gender equality, workplace dynamics, and social development. All answers will remain confidential and will be used for research purposes only.
